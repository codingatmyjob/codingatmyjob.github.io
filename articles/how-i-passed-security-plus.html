How I passed CompTIA Security+

October 26, 2025 Certification

Short version: structured study, lots of practice questions, hands-on labs, and consistent weekly time blocks.

Study plan

I split study into four 2-week blocks: fundamentals (networking + security basics), domains with hands-on labs, practice tests and gap review, and final exam prep. I scheduled 1.5–2 hours most weeknights and longer on weekends.

Resources

  • Official exam objectives — use as a checklist.
  • One or two video courses for guided learning.

Hands-on labs

Set up a small home lab (virtual machines) to practice configuring firewalls, ACLs, and PKI tasks. Real configuration experience made scenario questions easier.

Exam day tips

Get a good night's sleep, do a light review in the morning, and pace yourself on the exam — flag and return to questions if unsure. Read scenarios carefully and eliminate wrong answers first.

Final thoughts

Consistency beats cramming. Use practice tests to direct study and build confidence. Good luck.
